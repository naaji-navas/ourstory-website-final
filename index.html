Our Story

I always had an interest in shopify dropshipping, so in my leisure time I started learning more about it and in the process of setting up a store on shopify. In 2021, I started off with my first shopify business. But being an occasional dropshipper I faced certain issues. Manually importing reviews from other sites was a tiresome job, and many a time the imported reviews wouldn't be in sync with the current date. Also, I didn't have the option to upload photos in my reviews. We all know how important reviews are for a store owner as it provides customers the confidence they need to go through with their purchase. As I couldn't find a way to make this process easier, I decided to start Revyu to help solve these problems for other shopify store owners. My team and I noted all the problems store owners faced and all the ways in which we could help them manage their store with ease from the reviews perspective. Revyu contains all the features necessary for a shopify store owner to manage reviews, making Revyu a dream come true for all shopify dropshippers.
