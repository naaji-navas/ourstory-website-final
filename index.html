About Us

    We at Revyu, help all the Shopify store owners and dropshippers out there with a problem they constantly struggle with: Reviews. Reviews play a big role in e-commerce business, so big that it can make or break sales. With Revyu, customers can skip the step of framing proper sentences to write reviews. All they have to do is choose a few keywords, and a unique review for the product will be generated by the AI feature. Similarly, the store owners can cultivate a good relationship with their customers by responding to their reviews. Thus making the whole process a breeze for the owner and an interesting, one-of-a-kind experience for the customer.

Our mission

By bringing our AI powered software to startups and businesses, we strive to guarantee that they are always ahead of the game with up to date product and service reviews and ensure that our clients don't have to rely on another platform again

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a valuable long term review retrieving and modifying AI powered app, tailored to guide every entrepreneur to their purpose

Our Story

I always had an interest in shopify dropshipping, so in my leisure time I started learning more about it and in the process of setting up a store on shopify. In 2021, I started off with my first shopify business. But being an occasional dropshipper I faced certain issues. Manually importing reviews from other sites was a tiresome job, and many a time the imported reviews wouldn't be in sync with the current date. Also, I didn't have the option to upload photos in my reviews. We all know how important reviews are for a store owner as it provides customers the confidence they need to go through with their purchase. As I couldn't find a way to make this process easier, I decided to start Revyu to help solve these problems for other shopify store owners. My team and I noted all the problems store owners faced and all the ways in which we could help them manage their store with ease from the reviews perspective. Revyu contains all the features necessary for a shopify store owner to manage reviews, making Revyu a dream come true for all shopify dropshippers.
